---
title: "My Mood"
date: 2016-04-21
---

For some day, when i am down
Like a king, without a crown
Hug me tight, with all the brawn
Kiss me soft, and don't you frown

You see me lonely, come talk to me
Chained in thoughts, you set me free
Hold my hand and closer you be
Then note my smile and utmost glee.
